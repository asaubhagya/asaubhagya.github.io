Otter.ai: Cloud Power, but Privacy Tradeoffs

Otter.ai is a leader in live meeting transcription and has strong collaboration features. However, everything is processed in the cloud. Your recordings are uploaded, processed, and stored on Otter's servers—raising questions about privacy, data retention, and regulatory compliance for businesses or individuals.

Best for: Teams needing live transcription and collaboration.

Pricing: Free (300 min/mo), $16.99/mo or $120/yr Pro, unlimited plans for teams.

Otter's security details →

Apple Voice Memos: Private, But No AI

Voice Memos is Apple's built-in solution. Audio stays on your device, so it's private—but there is no transcription or AI. You get basic recording and sharing, nothing more.

Best for: Simple voice notes, no text needed.

Pricing: Free, included with iOS. No upgrades or AI options.

Rev Voice Recorder: Pay-Per-Minute, Cloud-Based

Rev Voice Recorder combines recording with human or AI-powered transcription, but all processing is in the cloud, and the main business is pay-per-minute transcription ($1.50/min for human, $0.25/min for AI). Good for occasional, professional-grade transcripts, but costs add up.

Best for: Users who need accurate, human-generated transcripts and are willing to pay.

Pricing: Free to record, then pay per transcription ($1.50/min human, $0.25/min AI).
