Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Everything stays on your device.

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Meetly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Audio Data

  • Microphone Access: Meetly requests access to your device's microphone to record meetings and conversations for transcription purposes.
  • Audio Processing: All audio recordings are processed locally on your device using on-device speech recognition technology.
  • Storage: Audio files and transcriptions are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to external servers.

Device Information

  • App Usage: We may collect basic app usage statistics to improve performance and user experience.
  • Device Type: Information about your device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes.

How We Use Information

  • Transcription: Audio data is used solely to provide speech-to-text transcription services.
  • AI Analysis: Transcribed text may be processed locally to generate meeting summaries and insights.
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us improve app functionality and performance.

100% On-Device Processing

All personal data, including audio recordings and transcriptions, is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your audio recordings or transcriptions to cloud servers.

  • Encryption: Data stored on your device is protected using iOS security features.
  • User Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete recordings at any time.

Data Sharing

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • User-Initiated Sharing: You may choose to export or share transcriptions, but this is entirely under your control.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Rights

Access

You can access all your data through the app interface.

Deletion

You can delete individual recordings or all data at any time.

Control

You can revoke microphone permissions through iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

Meetly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or other appropriate means.
