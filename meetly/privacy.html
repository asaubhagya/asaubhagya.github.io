Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last updated: May 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Meetly is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your voice recordings and personal data should remain yours. That's why we've built Meetly to process everything on your device, ensuring nothing leaves your control.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Meetly is built on a foundation of privacy-by-design. We deliberately do NOT collect:

  • Voice recordings or audio files
  • Transcribed text content
  • AI-generated summaries
  • Personal conversations or meeting content
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Contact information or personal details

2. On-Device Processing

All audio processing happens entirely on your device using:

  • OpenAI Whisper: Speech recognition runs locally on your device
  • Local AI Models: Summarization and analysis happen offline
  • Device Storage: All recordings stay in your device's secure storage
  • No Cloud Processing: Your data never leaves your device

3. Minimal Data Collection

We only collect the absolute minimum required for app functionality:

  • App Usage Analytics: Basic usage patterns to improve the app (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical diagnostics to fix bugs (no personal data)
  • Purchase Information: Only through Apple's App Store (we don't see payment details)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Since we don't collect your voice data, there's nothing sensitive to share. Any anonymized analytics are used solely for improving app performance and user experience.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Recordings: Remove any recording directly from the app
  • Export Data: Export your recordings in multiple formats
  • Opt-out Analytics: Disable usage analytics in app settings
  • Data Portability: Your data remains accessible to you at all times

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Local Encryption: All recordings are encrypted on your device
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in iOS secure keychain and sandboxed storage
  • No Network Transmission: Audio data never transmitted over the internet
  • Regular Updates: Security patches delivered through app updates

7. Children's Privacy

Meetly is designed to be safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information or voice data, there are no special privacy concerns for children. However, we recommend parental guidance for users under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
