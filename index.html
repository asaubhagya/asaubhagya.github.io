About Me

Started with Chemical Engineering at IIT Madras, but gradually found myself drawn to data and technology. After exploring consulting and entrepreneurship, discovered a passion for building systems that make sense of complex data.

Spent time at Zomato during an interesting growth phase, working on analytics and operations. Later joined Grab, where I've been focusing on data infrastructure and leading engineering teams across different regions.

Currently work on core data platforms—the kind of systems that handle large-scale data processing and help teams make better decisions. Enjoy the challenge of building reliable, scalable infrastructure.

What drives me: The intersection of design, data, and code to solve meaningful problems. Whether it's working on platforms at scale or building weekend projects, I find satisfaction in creating things that work well and matter to people.

Outside work: Building side projects, exploring new technologies, and occasionally writing about lessons learned along the way. Always learning, always curious.
